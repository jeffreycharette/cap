Consolidated Armor Products (CAP) is a fully integrated sole-source manufacturer offering a converged suite of transparent and opaque ballistic rated materials along with the following services:
Custom Fabrications

Over the past four decades, the management team of Consolidated Armor Products (CAP) has been developing a reputation as a global manufacturing leader providing hardened security solutions through the use of armoring technologies for buildings and vehicles. Through the consolidation of four different companies including the largest architectural glass manufacturer in the south, Consolidated Armor Products (CAP) develops and manufactures our own brand of transparent and opaque armor.

Design and Engineering

We take a holistic approach in the design and engineering process, ensuring all potential weaknesses are eliminated. Our carefully engineered ballistic materials are designed and manufactured to provide unparalleled stopping power at the lowest possible material weight and investment. Designed from the inside-out, our armor solutions are crafted not only to stop projectiles but also the dangerous effects of blast overpressure.

Independent Certifications

Consolidated Armor Products (CAP) has no product patents. We do have glass recipes that we have developed using products and processes that are available on the open market. These products and processes have been tested and certified as reliable and allows us to meet or exceed our customers’ expectations. Consolidated Armor Products is not satisfied with merely meeting mandatory test specifications. We have ensured that the stopping power of our products and solutions far exceeds the abilities of our competition. All of our products have been extensively tested at Department of Defense approved independent laboratories in a simulated real world environment. Consolidated Armor Products uses the standard created by Underwriters’ Laboratories (U.L.) of the United States.

The company will continue to meet and/or exceed our customers’ requirements.
